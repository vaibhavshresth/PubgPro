Mirado

The Mirado is a classic muscle car: a fast, four-seat coupe/sedan that’s limited to Miramar. It can be found around downtown areas and main city streets. Think of the Mirado as a compliment to the other unique vehicles on Miramar: The van is extremely sturdy, but slow and ineffective when taken off-road; The pickup is great for off-road travel; And the Mirado is the fastest way to blaze down a highway. On level roads, the Mirado's speed reach up to 152 km/h. PUBG categorizes this vehicle as a 'Sedan' [1] as they state "The Mirado is a classic muscle car: a fast, four-seat sedan that’s limited to Miramar." In real life it would be categorized as a coupe/sports car. The Mirado is based on the first generation Ford Mustang Mach 1
