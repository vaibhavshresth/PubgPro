Vehicles

boat

The PG-117 is a speedboat water type vehicle in BATTLEGROUNDS. The speedboat is a relatively quick moving craft, but there's a huge trade off as the boat has no cover or protection against ranged attacks. Because of this, while it's a great vehicle for navigating coastal features (and especially dealing with constricting play area), it is very dangerous to operate.
