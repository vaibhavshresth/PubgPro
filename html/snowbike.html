Vehicles

Snowbike


The Snow Bike (similar to the Snowmobile) is a off-road vehicle that is drivable exclusively on Vikendi map in BATTLEGROUNDS The sleekest ride for the ultimate freeride experience across the wintery terrain. This Snow Bike can chase down your opponents at breakneck speeds or you can take chill while you work on your snow jumps and flips. The Snow Bike is multi-purpose vehicle, geared to making you the nimblest of riders
