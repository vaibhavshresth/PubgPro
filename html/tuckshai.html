tuckshai


The Tukshai, more commonly called 'Tuk-tuk' within the Asia region or 'Auto rickshaw' in India and Pakistan, 'Bajaj' in Indonesia or 'CNG/auto rickshaw' in Bangladesh. Tuk-tuks are used for transportation needs to get around the city like Taxi's, but some countries like Thailand, Police use Tuk-tuks for patrol use in heavy populated areas where the Tuk-tuk can easily navigate.
