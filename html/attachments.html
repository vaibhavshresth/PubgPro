scopes


2x aimpoint


While this scope appears mediocre in regard to its zoom power and reticle, the reticle allows the user an improvised method of rangefinding - a standing person 100 meters away will fit perfectly between the top and bottom of the outer circle, and a standing person 200 meters away will fit between the center dot and the outer circle. At 300 meters, the width of a standing person will be obscured by the center dot.

3x sight


The 3x scope represents a compromise between the 2x and 4x scopes. The reticle can be used as a rangefinder for targets between 100 meters out to 400 meters by using the shoulder width of a target and matching it up with the horizontal lines. The reticle brightness can also be adjusted to current lighting conditions. Because the 3x scope's magnification sets the field of view to 26.66°,[1] players using a default field of view value higher than 80° will technically get slightly more than 3x magnification power due to the greater difference in FOV values.

4x acog


The ACOG scope allows for the user to engage targets at medium to long ranges. With a built in rangefinder, and its 4x zoom, the ACOG scope is one of the most useful attachments a player can come across, as such it is highly recommended to take it, even if you do not have a weapon that can accept it. As of the Miramar update, the 4x, along with Red Dot Sight and Holographic Sight, no longer have adjustable zeroing distance, setting the gun to a fixed 100 meter zero.

6x sight


The 6x scope is a rare world spawn, being the highest powered scope for guns that don't fall into the category of DMR or sniper rifle. The reticle also features a stadiametric rangefinder suitable for standing targets between 300 to 800 meters. This scope is very versatile due to its simple rangefinder and ability to zoom out to 3x magnification, as well as its compatibility with assault rifles and submachine guns, greatly extending the range at which opponents can be attacked at. Because the 6x scope's magnification sets the field of view to 13.33°,[1] players using a default field of view value higher than 80° will technically get slightly more than 6x magnification power due to the greater difference in FOV values.

8x CQBSS


The 8x CQBSS Scope is a sight type attachment in BATTLEGROUNDSThis variable power scope is a must-have for anyone wishing to engage targets at long ranges. It is ideal for weapons with high muzzle velocities as compensation of gravity and bullet speed is not required as much compared to weapons with slower bullet velocities. Scope sway may be an issue at further ranges, therefore it is recommended to hold one's breath before firing.

red dot sight


An open holographic sight that projects a red dot in the center of a glass or acrylic pane. It also speeds up the time it takes to aim down the sights by 20%. Compared to the holographic sight, the red dot sight has a less bulkier frame, although the reticle is somewhat blurrier. Nevertheless, the red dot sight is a good addition to any gun that can accept it as it has no major downsides over iron sights. Featuring adjustable reticle brightness and shape when not attached to a pistol, the red dot sight can be altered to suit the user's needs on the fly.

holographic sight


A reflex sight with a sharp reticle, although the bulky frame can reduce some situational awareness compared to the red dot sight. Recommended for closer range fights. The reticle's brightness can be adjusted by rolling the mouse wheel, and its color changed between either green or red with the zeroing keys. At 100 meters, a standing person will fit within the outer circle of the reticle, allowing for quick rangefinding.

muzzles


pistol suppressor


Pistols Suppressor is a muzzle attachment that helps to reduce the weapon sound and remove the muzzle flash when firing. Suppressor (Pistols) is commonly found in Ground Loots. Pistols Suppressor can attach with 5 weapons.

duckbill


Duckbill is a muzzle attachment for Shotgun that can help to reduce bullet spread and make the long shot easier when attached to the weapons. Duckbill is commonly found in World Spawn Items.

choke


Choke is a muzzle attachment for Shotguns and Pistol. It helps to reduce the bullet spread when firing. Choke is commonly found in World Spawn Items.

SNIPER RIFLES SUPPRESSOR


Sniper Rifles Suppressor is a muzzle attachment. It helps to reduce weapon fire sound and remove muzzle flash when firing. Sniper Rifles Suppressor can be found in World Spawn Items. This is the best attachment for Sniper Rifles because it requires stealth in order to confuse enemies and securing your own position after firing.

SMG SUPPRESSOR


SMG Suppressor is a muzzle attachment that reduces the sound of the weapon when firing and removes the muzzle flash when firing. Note: SMG Suppressor did not reduce weapon recoil or weapon damage. SMG Suppressor can be found in World Spawn Items.

ASSAULT RIFLES SUPPRESSOR


Assault Rifles Suppressor is a muzzle attachment. It reduces weapon firing sound and removes the muzzle flash when firing. Notes: Assault Rifles Suppressor did not reduce weapon damage and recoil. Assault Rifles Suppressor can be found in World Spawn Items.

SNIPER RIFLES FLASH HIDER


Sniper Rifles Flash Hider is a muzzle attachment. It helps to remove the muzzle flash and slightly reduces vertical and horizontal recoil when firing. Sniper Rifles Flash Hider can be found in World Spawn Items. It is very important for Sniper Rifles to have a flash hider attached, it can save you from getting pinned down when firing and make enemies harder to find you.

SMG FLASH HIDER


SMG Flash Hider is a muzzle attachment for Submachine Gun that eliminates muzzle flash when firing and slightly reduces horizontal and vertical recoils. Flash Hider (SMG) can be found in World Spawn Items. SMG Flash Hider is really helpful when fighting with enemies in the medium range. You can notice the enemy's flash easier when firing, so when equipping flash hider on your weapons enemies won't have an easy time trying to find you, also it helps to slightly reduce recoils as well.

ASSAULT RIFLES FLASH HIDER


Assault Rifles Flash Hider is a muzzle attachment. It helps to remove the muzzle flash when firing the weapons and slightly reduce vertical and horizontal recoils. Assault Rifle Flash Hider can be found in World Spawn Items.

SNIPER RIFLES COMPENSATOR


Sniper Rifles Compensator is a muzzle attachment. It helps to reduce vertical and horizontal recoils and improve weapon accuracy. Sniper Rifles Compensator can be found in World Spawn Items.

SMG COMPENSATOR


SMG Compensator is a muzzle attachment. Attach this item to Submachine gun to reduce vertical recoil and horizontal recoil so that you can control your spray easier. Compensator(SMG) is commonly found in World Spawn Items. When you find a compensator don't hesitate just put it on 1 of your weapon, it helps to improve your accuracy tremendously.

ASSAULT RIFLES COMPENSATOR


Assault Rifle Compensator is a muzzle attachment. It reduces horizontal recoil and vertical recoil. Assault Rifles Compensator can be found in World Spawn Items. When you find a compensator don't hesitate just put it on 1 of your weapon, it helps to improve your accuracy tremendously.








grips


HALF GRIP


Half Grip is a foregrip attachment that reduces recoil and increases recoil revovery, reduces stability when firing. Half Grip can attach to wide range of weapon types such as: Assault Rifle (AR) Designated Marksman rifle (DMR) Sub machinegun (SMG) Half Grip can be found in Ground Loot of any maps.

laser sight


Laser Sight is a foregrip attachment that reduces hip fire spread. Laser Sight is mostly use for pistols in other to gain more accuracy when firing. Laser Sight can attach to wide range of weapon types such as: Assault Rifle (AR) Designated Marksman rifle (DMR) Sub machinegun (SMG) Pistols Laser Sight can be found in Ground Loot of any maps.

thumb grip


Thumb Grip is a foregrip attachment that reduces scope opening time, slightly reduces vertiacl recoil and slightly increases stability. Thumb Grip can be used for both single fire and full auto spray. Thumb Grip can attach to wide range of weapon types such as: Assault Rifle (AR) Designated Marksman rifle (DMR) Sub machinegun (SMG) Thumb Grip can be found in Ground Loot of any maps.

LIGHT GRIP


Light Grip is a foregrip attachment that increases recoil recovery and stability. It help to maintain your weapon stable when spraying up to 10 bullets. Light Grip can be used for both single tap fire and full auto spray. Light Grip can attach to wide range of weapon types such as: Assault Rifle (AR) Designated Marksman rifle (DMR) Sub machinegun (SMG) Light Grip can be found in Ground Loot of any maps.

angled foregrip


Angled Foregrip is a foregrip attachment that reduce horizontal recoil and faster return to aiming. Angled Foregrip is mostly use in close range combat because of the providing advantage. Angled Foregrip can attach to wide range of weapon types such as: Assault Rifle (AR) Designated Marksman rifle (DMR) Sub machinegun (SMG) Angled Foregrip can be found Ground Loot of any maps.

vertical foregrip


Vertical Foregrip is a foregrip attachment that reduces Vertical recoil when single tap fire and full auto spray. Vertical Foregrip can attach to wide range of weapon types such as: Assault Rifle (AR) Designated Marksman rifle (DMR) Sub machinegun (SMG) Vertical Foregrip can be found in Ground Loot of any maps.
