basics

Helmets


Level-1 helmet


Motorcycle Helmet (Level 1) is a head armor covering players head from getting headshot instantly. It is a great helmet to wear during early game before you getting fully equipped. The condition of this item is common you can find it early in the game after you drop


level-2 helmet


Military Helmet (Level 2) is a head armor that covering players head from getting headshot instantly and die. Level 2 Helmet is a good helmet to have during middle game phase or late game because it can help to reduce a lot of damage. The condition of this item is common so you can find it easily but sometimes it can be hard.

level-3 helmet


Spetsnaz Helmet (Level 3) is a hard head armor that covering players head from getting instant headshot and die, this helmet even save from a headshot of sniper bullets as well. Level 3 Helmet can also be found inside Air Drops.





vests


Level-1 vest


Police Vest (Level 1) is a protective covering that allows players to survive small-arms gunfire. The Police Vest (Level 1) is also add extra 50 more capacity for you to add more items. The condition of this item is common to find. You can easily find this item on the ground when landing.


level-2 vest


Police Vest (Level 2) is a protective covering that allows players to survive medium arms gunfire. The Police Vest (Level 2) is also add extra 50 more capacity for you to add more items. The condition of this item is common to find. Sometime it might be a little bit hard to find and sometime it is easy to find

level-3 vest


Military Vest (Level 3) is a protective covering that allows players to survive huge arms gunfire continuously. The Military Vest (Level 3) is also add extra 50 more capacity for you to add more items. The condition of this item is rare to find in the ground and also available to find in Air Drops.
